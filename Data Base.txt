GLOSSARY: TECHNICAL ENGINEERING (EPC/GRID)
=============================================

Drawing documentation: Documentación de planos técnicos.

Master Document List (MDL): Lista maestra de entregables.

Transmittal: Documento oficial de envío de información técnica.

Design Review Register: Registro de revisión de diseño.

Action tracker: Seguimiento de tareas pendientes.

Stakeholder communication matrix: Matriz de comunicación con partes interesadas.

Single-line diagram: Esquema unifilar.

Supporting documents: Documentos de respaldo.

Deliverables: Entregables.

EPC Contract: Contrato de ingeniería, compras y construcción.

ICP: Proveedor de conexiones independiente.

RFP: Solicitud de propuesta.

TSO: Operador del sistema de transporte.

DNO: Operador de la red de distribución.

Engineering assurance: Garantía de ingeniería.

Site inspection: Inspección in situ.

Testing and Commissioning: Pruebas y puesta en marcha.

Energization process: Proceso de energización.

Structured template: Plantilla estructurada.

Current practices: Prácticas actuales.

Expected outcome: Resultado esperado.

Repository: Repositorio / Base de datos.

Standardized: Estandarizado.

Streamline: Optimizar / Simplificar.

Optimization: Optimización.

Implementation: Implementación.

Functionalities: Funcionalidades.

Supervision: Supervisión.

Identify improvements: Identificar mejoras.

Standardized building blocks: Bloques de construcción estandarizados.

Automating processes: Automatización de procesos.

Data analysis: Análisis de datos.

CONNECTORS & DISCOURSE MARKERS
==================================

In addition: Además.

Additionally: Adicionalmente.

Furthermore: Es más.

Moreover: Además (muy formal).

In parallel: En paralelo.

At the same time: Al mismo tiempo.

As well as: Así como.

That said: Dicho esto.

Having said that: Habiendo dicho lo cual.

However: Sin embargo.

Despite this: A pesar de esto.

Regardless: Independientemente de.

On the other hand: Por otro lado.

So far: Hasta ahora.

Currently: Actualmente.

At the moment: En este momento.

At this stage: En esta etapa.

Going forward: De ahora en adelante.

Moving forward: En el futuro.

In the short term: A corto plazo.

In the coming weeks: En las próximas semanas.

As a result: Como resultado.

Therefore: Por lo tanto.

Consequently: En consecuencia.

Due to: Debido a.

PROFESSIONAL PHRASE BANK (CATEGORIZED)
=========================================

[ENGINEERING & COORDINATION]

"From an engineering perspective, we need to review this."

"The design complies with local grid requirements."

"The contractor needs to incorporate our comments."

"We are validating the design assumptions."

"This needs to be confirmed with the TSO."

[COLLABORATION & TEAMWORK]

"We are working closely with the Tender team."

"This is being carried out in coordination with the Grid Managers."

"Let’s make sure we are all on the same page."

"We provide central support but are fully integrated."

[PROBLEMS & CHALLENGES]

"We are currently facing some challenges with the contractor."

"One of the main issues is the delay in receiving templates."

"This represents a technical risk for the project."

"We are addressing this issue as a priority."

PRESENTATION STRUCTURES (MONTHLY COURTS)
===========================================

[INTRODUCING TOPICS]

"The first point in this Monthly Court is..."

"The next item on the agenda is..."

"The main idea behind this initiative is..."

"The purpose of this point is to make you aware that..."

[EXPLAINING PROGRESS]

"So far, we have completed three workshops."

"This work is still in progress."

"We expect to finalize the series by May."

"I will keep you updated on the results."

[CLOSING TOPICS]

"That’s all for this point."

"That concludes the update on this topic."

"The key message is simple: we are one team."
